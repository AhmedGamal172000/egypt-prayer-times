# Privacy Policy — Egypt Prayer Times

**Last updated:** 27 May 2026

## Overview

Egypt Prayer Times is a Chrome extension that displays Muslim prayer times for cities in Egypt. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The only data stored by this extension is:

- **Your selected preferences** (city, calculation method, madhab, time format, language, theme) — stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`.
- **Cached prayer time data** — temporarily cached locally to provide offline functionality.

None of this data leaves your device. We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

## External Services

This extension fetches prayer time data from the **Aladhan API** (`api.aladhan.com`). When you use the extension, your selected city and country are sent to this API to retrieve prayer times. No personal identifiers are included in these requests.

Please refer to [Aladhan's Privacy Policy](https://aladhan.com/privacy) for information on how they handle API requests.

## Permissions

This extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

- **`storage`** — To save your preferences locally.
- **`alarms`** — To schedule background updates and prayer time notifications.
- **`notifications`** — To display prayer time alerts.
- **`host_permissions: https://api.aladhan.com/*`** — To fetch prayer times from the Aladhan API.
